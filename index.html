IIIT GUWAHATI

Indian Institute of Information Technology Guwahati (IIITG) is a premier institute of higher education located in Guwahati, the capital city of the northeastern Indian state of Assam. Established in 2013, IIITG is one of the 20 Indian Institutes of Information Technology (IIITs) established by the Indian Government as institutions of national importance.

YUVAAN

YUVAAN, the annual cultural festival of Indian Institute of Information Technology Guwahati, from its beginning in 2016 have seen the participation of various student groups and artists from every corner of the country. Our founding members believed in welfare of society that's why YUVAAN even being an escape from reality of the world supports a social cause, working toward a better world. YUVAAN is all about showcasing and nurturing talent.
